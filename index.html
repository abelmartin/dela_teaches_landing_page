ABOUT

Hello, I'm Dela

I’m an English teacher with over 10 years of experience teaching adults and kids. I hold a Master’s in TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages) and have taught English in schools, community centers, and online. After taking time off to raise my two boys, I returned to teaching English in 2019. I helped transition my church’s English program online during the pandemic and began working with students on reading and pronunciation. I love teaching  and seeing my students read, speak, and grow in English. Outside of class, I love reading books for book club, going for runs with my local running club, and spending time in the garden.

Making it Conversational

Turn what you read, hear, and experience into everyday conversation. We’ll work with books, articles, and real-life topics to help you:

  • Speak naturally and with ease

  • Express your ideas clearly

  • Build vocabulary that fits your life

Perfect for learners who want to speak more fluently and enjoy their conversations in English.

Pronunciation Coaching

Fine-tune your English sounds, word stress, and rhythm so your speech is clear, confident, and easy to understand. You’ll get:

  • Feedback on your speaking

  • Practice materials

  • Tools to improve clarity of speech

Perfect for learners who want to polish their accent or feel more confident when speaking in public or at work.

Book Talk with Dela

Join my book club – A community of language lovers reading books one chapter at a time. Through videos, short messages, audio clips, and conversation prompts, you’ll:

  • Enjoy reading without getting stuck

  • Grow your reading and speaking skills without feeling overwhelmed.

Perfect for learners who love to read and want to practice reading and speaking in a low-pressure way.
